About Us

SRMU Entrepreneurship Development Cell is the genesis for entrepreneurial activity at Shri Ramswaroop Memorial University. It aspires to develop culture for entrepreneurship on campus and create an ecosystem conducive to innovation.
Entrepreneurship Development Cell is a pioneer amongst the institutional e-cells of Lucknow. It provides inspiration, seeding , mentoring and training for Entrepreneurial activities.
Entrepreneurship Development Cell is a forum created by the students, of the students and for the students. It provides exponential peer- to- peer learning under the supervision of expert faculty guides and industry mentors. Students are encouraged to belive in their dreams and guided towards their actualisation. Assistance is provided in project management, scaling, talent acquisition, financing and all dimensions of initiating and sustaining a venture. .

Mission and Vision

Our Mission
The mission of the Entrepreneurship Development Cell is to foster entrepreneurial thinking, promote business knowledge and inculcate confidence in every student. It involves promoting the establishment of one’s own enterprise through the inculcation of leadership skills , creativity, project management and problem solving skills.
Our aim is to enhance and promote entrepreneurial culture within the institutional mechanism by providing motivation, guidance and opportunity to students as well as prospective entrepreneurs.

Our Vision
To promote Entrepreneurship by facilitating innovative minds to paint their dreams on the canvas of reality with the objective of converting them into successful ventures. To maximize the potential of each individual and thereby contributing to national progress. .

Dr.Jyoti Dewan is a practicing entrepreneur with hands-on experience in the retail sector and switched to academics after a successful career in the industry. She has created a niche in the arena of Entrepreneurship by the launch and successful completion of real life projects and is associated with several consultancy projects for start-ups. She specialises in branding and image management.
She has received special training in ‘The Future of Marketing’ from Dr. Philip Kotler, distinguished Professor of International Marketing at the Northwestern University Kellogg Graduate School of Management, Chicago. She was founding Dean- Faculty of Management & Commerce at SRMU and initiated the department from ground zero. Creation of syllabi and generating templates for various processes was her key responsibility area.
She founded the Entrepreneurship Cell at Shri Ramswaroop Memorial University and is keenly involved with teaching courses in Communications, Marketing, Entrepreneurship & Corporate Governance. Under her leadership collaborations have been created with Entrepreneurship Cell IIT-Kanpur, Entrepreneurship Cell-IIT Mumbai, National Innovation Foundation and the National Entrepreneurship Network- Wadhwani Foundation for imparting cutting edge skills and mentorship to aspiring entrepreneurs. Under her leadership networks have been developed with The Indus Entreprenuership (TiE), Confederation of Indian Industries (CII) and Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce (FICCI) for providing real life cases and insights from the world of business and entrepreneurship.
Due to her proactive approach the Entrepreneurship Cell (SRMU) has been consistently spinning entrepreneurship related talks and competitions. She has mentored groups of inter-disciplinary students who are passionate about the cause and assist in the organization of the events. This goes a long way in building their knowledge and confidence for challenges ahead. SRMU’s first Entrepreneurship week featuring talks, competitions and games on the topic was organized in February, 2018. SRMU’s first Entrepreneurship Summit was on held 20th-21st April, 2018 in association with TiE (The Indus Entrepreneurs), NHRDN (National Human Resource Development Network) and the Wadhwani Foundation wherein a bevy of illustrious speakers addressed the delegates.
Dr.Jyoti Dewan is also keenly involved with research in the domain of Entrepreneurship. Research paper written by her, titled “Small and Medium Enterprises: Experiences and Imperatives in the Process of Internationalization” was adjudged the best case of the International Conference on ‘Transnational Entrepreneurs & International SMEs in Emerging Economies : Drivers and Strategies’, May 20-22, 2015 for which she was awarded a certificate and trophy, by Indian Institute of Management –Bangalore and Lucas College and Graduate Business School, San Jose University, California, USA. Research Papers written by her have also been published in Springer Nature International. Recently a proposal submitted by her was also shortlisted for development of Entrepreneurship MOOCs by UGC under the PG Pathshala initiative.
In February, 2018, Jyoti received certificate of appreciation from the Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh for supporting FICCI Flo- IWAAF (Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce Ladies Organization-International Women Artisans Awadh Festival) 2018, promoting skill development and entrepreneurship among rural women. She also received certificate of appreciation from National Textiles Minister- Smriti Irani for her dedicated efforts and support for FICCI Flo International Women Artisans Awadh Festival and Film Forum (14-16 Nov, 2018). Jyoti is heading the Swayam Chapter of FICCI Flo Lucknow-Kanpur Chapter which works for promoting women entrepreneurs.
In June 2017, clearing a rigorous interview and written process, Jyoti was successful in earning TEDx license for Shri Ramswaroop Memorial University. Since then, she has worked to successfully conceptualise and execute TEDxSRMUWomen and TEDxSRMU events in November 2017 and January 2018 respectively. TED Talks is an NGO that promotes innovation under the tagline of ‘Ideas Worth Sharing’.
A six month certification course in Entrepreneurship was launched in collaboration with ISOEL (International School of Entrepreneurial Leadership) a USA based NGO wherein American Entrepreneurs flew down to coach students at rock bottom cost. Rural orientation is provided to students via visit to the Deva hamlet. Outstation field visits are also conducted to expose students to the industrial environment. ‘My Story Sessions’ are organized wherein achievers are invited to the campus to share their journey with our students. Jyoti has collaborated International certification program in Entrepreneurship endorsed by Seattle-Pacific University to provide students with cutting edge. She has also collaborted with University of Carleton-Canada for promoting value added courses in Entrepreneurship for students and faculty.
Jyoti graduated with honours from the prestigious Lady Shri Ram College, Delhi University and was conferred the Gold Medal for excellence in academics for her MBA Program at Lucknow University. She is an avid speaker and presenter. She is the founding member of Lucknow Chapter of Confederation of Indian Industry-Yi & Lucknow Chapter FICCI Ladies Organization. She is also an active member of Lucknow Management Association (LMA) and Lucknow Chapter of The Indus Entrepreneurs (TiE).
Jyoti is adept at leveraging social networks for the benefit of underprivileged. She has served on the board of prestigious Loreto Alumnae Committee for several years and was also the Chairlady of Lucknow Ladies Circle- part of an international network, which works for promoting goodwill and empowerment of marginalized women.

E-Summit

The Industrial and Collaboration Cell, SRMU, organised the Entrepreneurship Summit (E-Summit) on 20th-21th April, 2018 - a confluence of industry veterans, business leaders, entrepreneurs, visionary, students and individuals who have the vision of building the next big thing.
This summit was the preeminent annual gathering of entrepreneurs , investors , industry heads and academicians who shared their ideas , experience and views on the topic related to business.
This summit provided a comprehensive coverage of the following areas:

  • The Entrepreneurial Perspective
  • To identifying the legalities involved in any business
  • To identifying the legalities involved in any business
  • Finances
  • Management, Growth of closure of the business

Objective
The Entrepreneurship Summit (E-Summit) is the annual flagship event of the entrepreneurship cell. The basic objective of E-summit is to inspire youth to dream, challenge and develop entrepreneurial ventures. The Summit also spread information on ‘Startup India’ an initiative of the Government of India.
The action plan of this initiative was based on the three pillars of :
  • Simplification and Handholding
  • Funding Support and Incentives
  • Industry-Academia Partnership and Incubation.
